My Story

In 2013, I embarked on my software engineering journey, writing my first Java program to calculate the area of a triangle, a small step that ignited my passion for technology. Today, I stand as a full-stack software engineer with a track record of impactful projects, ranging from web, mobile and games.

  • I developed a USSD payment application using Python and Django, adopted nationally in Tanzania for DStv, processing over 2.5K+ transactions daily.
  • I aggregated various mobile and banking payment services in Tanzania (Vodacom, Airtel, Tigo, Halotel, and Selcom) into a robust API gateway, facilitating payments for the MyDStv app with 5M+ downloads.
  • I ventured into game development, creating entertaining experiences using Java, Libgdx, and Godot.
  • As an Associate Android Developer, my Android app development skills, powered by Kotlin, contributed to a distributed network of charging stations in Tanzania and Nigeria, providing power solutions for entire homes and communities.
  • During my spare time, I designed a personal Android app in Kotlin for playing podcasts from the internet.
  • My dedication to excellence earned me the title of Best Student for four consecutive years.

These accomplishments reflect my commitment to pushing boundaries and delivering innovative solutions across various domains of software engineering.
